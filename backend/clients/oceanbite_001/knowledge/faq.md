**Is there Shellfish in everything?**
Many of our dishes contain shellfish. If you are allergic, please ask for our **"Land & Earth"** menu section which is prepared with separate utensils, though cross-contamination is always a risk in our open kitchen.

**Do you accept walk-ins?**
Yes, walk-ins are welcome! However, for Friday nights and weekends, we highly recommend booking a table in advance.

**Are you Halal?**
